Executive Biography

Upperhill College of Professional Studies was established to systematically redefine technical professional training profiles. It was born out of a critical operational mission: to replace traditional, purely theoretical learning templates with robust, industry-driven Technical and Vocational Education Training (TVET).

Academic Background

My passion for computing and academic equity shaped my personal path. I began my training foundation in technical education through a specialized diploma program, and subsequently advanced my skills in higher education. I'm holding a degree in Information Communication Technology (ICT).

Origin Story & Vision

Founded on January 23, 2025, Upperhill College was built on an innovative framework: the Global Full Scholarship Program. Under this system, the scholarship covers a student's core tuition costs, leaving them responsible only for statutory fees. This framework has successfully empowered over 2,000 students nationwide, clearing paths to professional careers.

"True leadership isn't about setting rules; it's about engineering platforms where young talent discovers its capacity to innovate, create, and lead."
